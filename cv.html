Operations, Development and Transformation Manager

NHS Primary Care

My current chapter is about operational grip in a setting where patient experience, workflow control, reporting accuracy and financial discipline all matter at once.

  • Lead operational performance, transformation and service control across clinical, administrative and patient-facing functions, supporting delivery across a £1.56m annual income base.
  • Improved patient experience by increasing Google review score from 2.09 to 4.51 and raising five-star reviews from 19.8% to 81.3% across 209 tenure-period reviews.
  • Supported a £149.9k improvement in profit before appropriation, released £33.3k of working capital through a 40.3% reduction in debtor balances and helped increase cash at bank by £172.6k year on year.
  • Built senior reporting across workflow, room utilisation, payroll and contractual income, including reconciliation of 64 PCSE remittance files covering £2.83m.

Operations Manager

Copper Alloys Ltd

In manufacturing, the focus shifted toward production discipline, delivery reliability, capacity and the practical mechanics of operational resilience.

  • Improved operational resilience through changes to processes, equipment, capacity and ways of working, supporting business growth in a precision manufacturing setting.
  • Used sales, delivery and KPI reporting to monitor performance, maintain customer service levels and strengthen execution across the operation.
